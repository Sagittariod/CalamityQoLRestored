Re-introduces many different features Calamity used to have which gave power to the player through various means. All Quality of Life features are configurable and up to date with the latest Calamity version.

Mod icon by Zenovia.


>- Overview -<

- Reintroduced removed items from the Starter Bag's former loot pool
- Restored recipes for a plethora of vanilla items
- Reintroduced many removed items from various Town NPC shops
- Reintroduced removed items from the loot pools of multiple Calamity biome crates
- Various item drop rates from enemies are increased
- Made event items once again non-consumable
- Expert-exclusive drops are once again available in all difficulties
- Reintroduced a special interaction with the Angler which allowed for obtaining a Golden Fishing Rod consistently
- Reintroduced Terraprisma to Empress of Light's common weapon drop pool
- Reintroduced Angling Kits
- Made Reaver Shark once again have increased pickaxe power
- Made Ankh Charm used in the Asgard's Valor recipe and returned debuff immunities to shields in its line
- Increased the drop rate of fruits from trees
- Made Cosmolight obtainable in early hardmode

This mod introduces other optional changes not included by Calamity in any version, all of which default to being off. These are listed below:

- Added a recipe for Bakidon to be obtainable late into pre-hardmode
- Added the ability for non-legendary weapons dropped by a given boss to be shimmered into one another


Feel free to pitch other re-additions you'd like to see!
